Intro

Welcome to my blog for Dev Academy 2024.

I have styled this blog in a style that I feel is similar to my interests. I have chosen an 8-bit/classic video game font style for my main headers and a controller to represent my interest in gaming.

To represent my interest in stand-up comedy a more traditional / simple style for the core content symbolizing getting the point across (if you can't do that when telling a joke you're in trouble!) along with a microphone in a spotlight.

Throughout the duration of the course I will be updating this site with blog posts of my journey. I'm sure this blog with update and change over time.

To access the blog posts you can click on the relevant course segment to find links through to each entry. To return to this page you can click the Dev Academy Blog 2024 banner at the top
