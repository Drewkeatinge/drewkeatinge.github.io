Margin, Border, and Padding

What's the difference?

When I think about Margin, Borders and Padding, I think about swimming pools.

To help illustrate my analogy I have made up this image below labelling each part with what represents what.

Padding

The padding is like the area around the pool where you can walk around, or put a deck chair & tables etc it's the space between the element (in this case the pool) and the border (the fence)

Border

The border here is represented by the fence, - this is the boundry of the element and is what sperates it from other elements. Just as the walls of the pool hold the water, the border is the walls that hold the element.

Margin

This is the area around the element, it's the space between the border and other elements. in this example it would be the distance you walk from your house to your swimming pool.
